Personal Finance For Everyone

What is Personal Finance and Why It Matters?

Have you ever felt like your money just disappears at the end of every month and you have no idea where it went ?

While some people manage it very well.Well, that's where personal finance comes in. In this talk, I'll break down what personal finance really means, why it's crucial for everyone, and how you can start taking control of your money right now.

Personal finance is all about managing your money — from budgeting and saving to investing and planning for the future. It’s the skill that helps you control your finances rather than letting your finances control you.
